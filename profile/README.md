## 👋 About Puntu Studio

Puntu Studio is a one-person indie game studio from **Pokhara, Nepal** — built on a foundation of 12 years of civil engineering and architectural design, now fully channeled into game development and 3D art.

Behind the studio is **Subodh Man Singh Bhandari** — transitioning from designing buildings and commercial showrooms across Pokhara into building games, crafting Blender assets, and documenting every step of the journey.

This page is where the games live. The devlogs, the wins, the failures, and everything in between are on YouTube and Instagram at **@thepuntustudio**.
